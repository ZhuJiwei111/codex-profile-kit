## Humanizer

- Repository: https://github.com/blader/humanizer
- Fixed commit: 1b48564898e999219882660237fde01bf4843a0f
- License: MIT, Copyright 2025 Siqi Chen.

Retained ideas: judge prose patterns in context, calibrate observable voice
features from an authorized sample, and avoid word-level or punctuation-level
false positives. Rejected ideas include detector claims, rigid word bans, and
invented personality or experience.

## Anti-Defensive Writing

- Repository: https://github.com/Kiterlin/anti-defensive-writing
- Fixed commit: 088df470b2871a66315698cd55b6a9fd0301d918
- License: MIT, Copyright 2026 Kiterlin.

Retained ideas: distinguish necessary boundaries from rhetorical defense and
prefer direct positive scope only when propositionally equivalent. Preserve
limitations, uncertainty, and meaningful negation.
